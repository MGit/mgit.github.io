Martin Guay

I lead R&D at the intersection of graphics, AI and human-computer interaction. In 2018, I established marker-less motion capture capabilities and in-camera VFX called synth2track used by Industrial Light & Magic (ILM) and Marvel. More recently, I established Deep Motion Authoring which renders creating natural motion much more accessible. Prior to this, I pioneered autonomous characters driven by voice, terrain and music, to enable conversational interactions, digital bands that can play any song and 3D character agents that navigate environments.

During my Ph.D. I ellucidated artistic principles that help artists establish clear visual communication when drawing, and turned these into new sketch-based interfaces for 3D motion authoring. I started my career in video game production developping game engines dfor different platforms such as the XBox, PC and iPhone.
